How it works

Three snaps to a healthier you

Just snap, review, and track. Pip handles the math.

1

📸 Snap your meal

Open the camera and point at your plate. Pip's AI identifies every food item instantly.

2

🥗 Review the estimate

Pip shows calories, protein, carbs, and fat. Tap to edit if you added a little extra cheese.

3

🔥 Track your progress

Your daily dashboard rings update, streaks grow, and trends build automatically.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don’t

A calorie tracker that respects your privacy and works anywhere.

🤖

AI Photo Scan

Gemma 4 on-device AI identifies food and estimates calories, protein, carbs, and fat from a single photo.

🏆

Streaks & Goals

Personalized daily calorie goals based on your age, weight, height, and activity level. Keep your streak alive.

📷

Barcode Scanner

Scan barcodes on packaged foods for instant nutritional info. No typing required.

✍️

Manual Entry

Quickly log meals by typing a description. Pip estimates the rest using AI.

📈

Trends & Diary

Browse your meal history, track weight changes, and spot patterns in your nutrition over time.

🔋

Fully Offline

Zero network requests. No photos ever leave your phone. No servers, no accounts, no tracking.

Your privacy
